How startups are solving the existing issues of Intra City Transport Logistics?


Introduction

As we venture further in the century, we are seeing a lot of technological advancements. Advancements and solutions solving age-old issues and problems. The recent upturn in startup culture has made people’s lives easier as their needs are getting catered to.

One such issue that needs attention is the intra city transport logistics sector. Now, a lot of startups have been catering to solving the existing logistical issues, but a lot needs to be done still. The unorganised transport sector is still highly prevalent in India, and it seems like we are behind schedule to make this thing easier for users.
There are many companies trying to solve the existing problems of the sector, and they are on the right path.


What are the issues faced by the people?

In the unorganised scenario, a vendor booking transport for their goods still has to negotiate for the price. He/She still has to wait for hours looking for the delivery partner to pick up the goods as he is busy delivering some other order. After hours of haggling for price and waiting for the partner to reach, he/she now has to worry about loading the goods into the transport. Even after that, he/she is stressed about the safety and security of the consignment. There are deeper issues involved for people that require industry-specific transportation services.

These issues are only the start. It is much more difficult for common folks to find a vehicle to move their goods. They have to pay insane amounts to local movers and packers.

To solve such issues, the nation’s startup ecosystem is working towards creating a solution that will make lives easy. We have seen a number of startups coming up with innovative solutions.


What benefits are people getting?

Benefits for the user
Hassle-Free Booking: One can book the required vehicle anytime, anywhere without having to take the stress of the issues listed above. Transparent Pricing: People now do not have to haggle for price or wonder whether the driver will charge extra than the pre-agreed price. Realtime Tracking: No ambiguity as to whether the driver is on the right path or not. Also solves the issue of security. Safety: Only experienced and professional drivers with background checks are hired. Facilitates Growth: The ease in transportation facilitates growth among B2B clients.

Benefits for the drivers
It also helps the drivers get regular work. As the work is regular, they have control over when and where they want to work. They can accept and reject rides as per their liking. Also, the system has made it easy for them to get the money in their bank accounts whenever they want.


Conclusion

All in all the intra city transport logistic sector is seeing a huge advancement. This is bringing a revolution in the unorganised transport logistic sector. A lot needs to be done, but it's a start. As the sector gets organised, we can see small businesses growing. It will in turn boost the per capita income of the nation. People will find it easier to start a business as the supply chain issues are nullified by these innovative startups.
