MoveEasy:-
MoveEasy service allows users to deliver goods in a single drop location with single pickup location which possibly makes fast and instant delivery

MoveMulti :-
Multiple drop service allows users to deliver goods on multiple locations with single pick-up location.
Multiple pickup service allows users to choose multiple pickup location for goods with single drop location

MoveHire :-
MoveHire service allows users to rent available vehicle for specific time period, enables users to work according to their time and desire, the charges will be according to duration of time in addition to per kilometer charge.

MoveSuper:-
MoveSuper allows users to book a vehicle with a close container which gives users the surety to deliver their goods with safety and security
